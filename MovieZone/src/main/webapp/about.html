Welcome to MovieZone!!!

MovieZone is a place where you can store your favorite movies and search for any movie. MovieZone can also provide you with the newly released movie list and the most popular movies, and it can recommend movies based on the movies you like.

MovieZone is built by Ivy Jin. Ivy Jin has been a movie buff since she was a kid. She watches all kinds of movies. Her favorite movies are Inception, Blade Runner, Bohemian Rhapsody, and a lot more. She loves CS and loves building web apps. She has always been wanting to combine her passion for art, math, and CS, and this is her first step.
